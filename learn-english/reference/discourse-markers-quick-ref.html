Discourse Markers & Linking Words

Quick Reference Card · From Lesson 11

Adding Information (补充)

WordRegisterExample
AlsoInformalWe need more budget. We also need more people.
In addition (to)NeutralIn addition to the meeting, we have a call at 5.
FurthermoreFormalThe system is fast. Furthermore, it's easy to use.
MoreoverFormalShe has skills. Moreover, she understands business.

Contrast (转折)

WordRegisterExample
ButInformalIt's cheap, but it has fewer features.
HoweverFormalIt's cheap. However, it has fewer features.
AlthoughBothAlthough it's cheap, it has fewer features.
NeverthelessFormalThe data was incomplete. Nevertheless, we proceeded.
On the other handBothRemote saves costs. On the other hand, it reduces connection.

Cause & Effect (因果)

WordRegisterExample
SoInformalWe changed the plan, so the timeline shifted.
ThereforeFormalWe changed the plan. Therefore, the timeline shifted.
As a resultNeutralWe lacked data. As a result, we made assumptions.
ConsequentlyFormalMembers left. Consequently, the launch was delayed.
Due to / Because ofBothDue to bad weather, the flight was cancelled.

Sequencing (顺序)

WordUseExample
First / Second / FinallyListsFirst define the problem. Second, explore solutions.
MeanwhileConcurrentWe're updating the site. Meanwhile, marketing prepares.
SubsequentlySequentialThe investigation ended. Subsequently, policies changed.
In conclusion / To sum upSummaryIn conclusion, I recommend option B.

Clarifying & Emphasizing (澄清与强调)

WordUseExample
In factEmphasisThe budget was tight. In fact, it was below request.
ActuallyCorrectionI thought it was at 3. Actually, it's at 3:30.
In other wordsRephraseThe ROI is low. In other words, not worth it.
ImportantlyHighlightImportantly, findings were consistent across regions.
关键提醒：不要在同一个句子中同时使用 "Because" 和 "Therefore/So"。正确："Because it rained, we stayed home." 或 "It rained; therefore, we stayed home." 错误："Because it rained, therefore we stayed home."
