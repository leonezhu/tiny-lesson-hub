Collocations Quick Reference

Quick Reference Card · Professional Writing

Decision & Action

make a decision做决定 — never "do a decision"
take action采取行动
take responsibility承担责任
make a suggestion提建议 — not "give a suggestion"
make an effort做出努力 — not "perform an effort"
make a mistake犯错误 — not "commit a mistake"

Communication & Meetings

raise an issue提出问题
bring up a point提出要点
reach an agreement达成协议
provide feedback提供反馈
follow up with someone与某人跟进
give feedback给反馈 — "give" works with feedback

Data & Analysis

draw a conclusion得出结论 — not "make a conclusion"
gain insights获得洞察
meet a deadline满足截止日期
meet expectations满足期望
conduct research进行研究（正式）
do research做研究（非正式）

Problem Solving

resolve an issue解决问题（正式）
solve a problem解决问题（通用）
address a concern回应关切
implement a solution实施解决方案

Common Adjective Collocations

heavy rain大雨 — not "big rain"
fast food快餐 — not "quick food"
strongly recommend强烈推荐（正式）
really recommend强烈推荐（口语）
deeply concerned非常担忧
highly likely极有可能
