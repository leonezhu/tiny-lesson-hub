习语 vs 搭配

习语（Idiom）— 含义不可从字面推断

  • get the ball rolling — 开始做某事
  • on the same page — 意见一致
  • touch base — 简短联系
  • circle back — 回头再讨论
  • the bottom line — 关键要点
  • break the ice — 打破沉默/消除尴尬
  • raining cats and dogs — 倾盆大雨

搭配（Collocation）— 字面可理解，但必须这样组合

  • make a decision / a mistake / progress / money / a plan / an effort
  • do homework / research / business / a favor / exercise
  • ask a question (× say/tell a question)
  • take a look / a break / a seat / a photo (× make a look)
  • give advice / feedback / permission (× take feedback = 接受)
  • have a meeting / a discussion (× make/did a meeting)

高频错误速查

  • opened the computer → turned on the computer
  • did research → did research ✓ （注意：research 用 do！）
  • made research → did research ✓
  • said me the news → told me the news ✓
  • make a meeting → have a meeting ✓
  • did the meeting → had the meeting ✓
  • I learned the knowledge → I acquired the knowledge ✓

Make vs Do — 速记口诀

MAKE = 创造性、抽象结果（做出来一个新的东西）

DO = 执行性、体力性（执行一个已有的任务）

  • make a plan（创造） vs do the plan（执行？不，英语不用这个搭配）
  • make a speech（创造） vs do exercise（执行）
  • make a mistake（产生一个错误） vs do nothing（执行"什么也不做"）
